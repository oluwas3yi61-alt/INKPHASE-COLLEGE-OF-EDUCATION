Admissions

Undergraduate Admissions

Requirements:

  • 5 O'Level credits including English & Mathematics
  • Minimum JAMB score of 180 (200+ for competitive programs)
  • Pass Post-UTME screening
  • Valid birth certificate or age declaration
Apply Now

Postgraduate Admissions

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum CGPA of 3.0/5.0
  • Relevant field of study for intended program
  • Two recommendation letters from academic referees
  • Statement of purpose
