Our Government Partnership Journey

Inkphase College of Education began its journey as a pioneering institution in teacher education and development. Since its founding, the college has maintained a commitment to academic excellence and community service. In recognition of our outstanding contributions to education and our robust infrastructure, the state government has officially adopted us as a university. This transition represents a significant milestone that enhances our capacity to offer advanced degrees, conduct cutting-edge research, and expand our impact on national development. As Inkphase University, we continue our mission with renewed vigor, backed by government support and accountability.
