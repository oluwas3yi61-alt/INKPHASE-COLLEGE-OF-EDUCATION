Admissions

Step-by-step guide for prospective students. Apply online or visit our admissions office.

Admission Process

  1. 1

    Register for UTME/JAMB

    Apply through JAMB portal using Inkphase University code: 1234

  2. 2

    Post-UTME Screening

    Register online and pay screening fee of â‚¦2,500

  3. 3

    Admission Letter

    Successful candidates receive admission letter via email

  4. 4

    Registration & Payment

    Pay acceptance fee and register for courses

Eligibility Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Minimum 5 credits in WAEC/NECO/GCE including English & Mathematics
  • JAMB score of at least 180 for most programs (200+ for competitive programs)
  • Pass Post-UTME screening test

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum CGPA of 3.0/5.0 (Second Class Upper)
  • Relevant field of study for Master's/Ph.D. programs
  • Entrance examination and interview

Required Documents

Completed application form
Recent passport photograph (4x4)
Birth certificate or age declaration
Academic transcripts (O'Level & A'Level)
JAMB result slip
Recommendation letters (for postgraduate)
